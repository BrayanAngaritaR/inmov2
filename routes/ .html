As a small but mighty nonprofit, Curriki has a very lean operation, dedicated to building technology and community. We rely on grants and donations from individuals and organizations who are impassioned by our mission and impact. In addition, we earn revenue through supportive services for Certified Providers who support customers using CurrikiStudio. Funds are spent responsibly on programs and initiatives that will have the broadest impact on advancing our mission.

Your donation supports our non-profit mission, which is to bring free and open technology tools into every school, college and nonprofit organization.
